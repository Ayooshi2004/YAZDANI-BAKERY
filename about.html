Our Story

Yazdani Bakery was founded in 1953 by Ardeshir Yazdani, who brought his family recipes and baking techniques from Persia to start a new life. What began as a small corner bakery quickly gained a reputation for exceptional quality and authentic flavors.

For over 70 years, we've maintained our commitment to traditional baking methods, quality ingredients, and the warm, personal service that has been our hallmark since the beginning.

Today, the third generation of the Yazdani family continues to honor our heritage while innovating to meet contemporary tastes and preferences.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our bakery's history

1953

The Beginning

Ardeshir Yazdani opens the first Yazdani Bakery location with a small wood-fired oven and a handful of family recipes.

1968

Expansion

Following growing demand, we moved to a larger location and expanded our product range to include more European-style pastries.

1982

Second Generation

Ardeshir's son, Farhad Yazdani, takes over the family business and introduces new equipment while preserving traditional methods.

2001

Award-Winning

Yazdani Bakery wins its first national recognition for "Best Artisan Bakery" and begins supplying local restaurants and hotels.

2012

Third Generation

Cyrus Yazdani joins the family business after culinary training abroad, bringing new ideas while honoring family traditions.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

We continue to grow while staying true to our roots, serving our community with the same passion and quality that started it all.
