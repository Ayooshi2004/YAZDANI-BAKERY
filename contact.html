Directions

From Downtown: Head north on Main Street for 1.5 miles. Turn right onto Baker Street. We are located on the right side, 3 blocks down.

Public Transit: Take Bus #42 or #56 to the Baker Street stop. We are just across the street.

Parking: Free parking is available in our lot behind the building or on the street.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer local delivery for orders over $30 within a 10-mile radius. Please place your order at least 24 hours in advance for delivery. For special events and large orders, we can arrange delivery outside our usual area.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For standard custom cakes, we recommend placing your order at least 72 hours (3 days) in advance. For wedding cakes and more elaborate designs, we suggest 2-4 weeks notice to ensure we can accommodate your request and schedule a tasting if desired.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer various options for different dietary needs. We have gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free items available by special order. Please note that our kitchen does process allergens such as wheat, nuts, dairy, and eggs, so we cannot guarantee against cross-contamination.

Can I place an order online?

Currently, we accept orders via phone, email, or in person. For custom orders and special requests, we prefer to discuss details directly to ensure we can meet your exact needs. Please contact us at (555) 123-4567 or info@yazdanibakery.com to place your order.
